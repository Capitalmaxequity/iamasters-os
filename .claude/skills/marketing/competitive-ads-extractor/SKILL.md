# Competitive Ads Extractor

This skill extracts and analyzes competitor ads to identify working messaging, pain points, and creative patterns.

## Core Capabilities

1. **Extract Ads**: Scrapes ads from Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.
2. **Analyze Messaging**: Identifies problems, use cases, and value props.
3. **Identify Patterns**: Finds common successful creative and copy approaches.
4. **Competitor Comparison**: Compares multiple competitors to find market gaps.

## Usage Examples

- "Extract all current ads from [Competitor] on Facebook Ad Library."
- "Scrape ads from [Company] and analyze their B2B positioning strategy."
- "Compare ad approaches for these 5 competitors: [list]."

## Analysis Report Structure

- **Themes**: What topics do they emphasize (Productivity, Savings, etc.)?
- **Pain Points**: What specific problems are they highlighting?
- **Creative Patterns**: Video vs static, split screens, social proof.
- **Copy Hooks**: Which headlines and CTA patterns appear most frequently?
- **Audience**: Who are they targeting (Startups vs Enterprise)?

## Best Practices

- **Inspiration, not Plagiarism**: Use insights to inform original creative, never copy ads directly.
- **Track over time**: Monitor monthly to see how strategy evolves.
- **Platform Differences**: Note how messaging differs between LinkedIn (B2B) and Facebook (B2C).
- **Segments**: Identify different messages for different target audiences.
