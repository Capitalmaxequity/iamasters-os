# Video Downloader

Tool for fetching videos from YouTube and other streaming platforms for offline use.

## Core Capabilities

1. **Multi-Source**: Fetch from YouTube, Vimeo, and other common platforms.
2. **Quality Selection**: Choose resolutions ranging from 480p to 4K.
3. **Audio-Only**: Option to extract audio tracks (e.g., MP3/WebM) from video sources.
4. **Batch Mode**: Download entire playlists or multiple links simultaneously.
5. **Metadata**: Persists video title, description, and thumbnail during the download.

## Commands
- "Download this YouTube video in 1080p: [URL]"
- "Extract the audio as MP3 from this video: [URL]"
- "Download all videos in this playlist: [URL]"

## Safety & Compliance
- **Copyright**: Respect the creator's rights and platform terms of service.
- **Personal Use**: Use for archival or offline reference purposes.
- **Fair Use**: Ensure compliance with local laws regarding content repurposing.
